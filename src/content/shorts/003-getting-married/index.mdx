I got married in December 2025 to my beautiful girlfriend of 4 years, with a little wedding ceremony to take place in June. Highschool Xetera would be in disbelief, mostly because he didn't think it would ever happen. It's honestly such an incredible feeling to have found the right someone you want to spend your life with.

She was the only thing keeping me anchored to Turkey for the past 4 years. Now we have a shared goal of leaving the country to start a new life abroad. That will be a first for her, which would understandably be a scary feeling for anyone, but it's something I just can't wait for. I've never felt at home here.
